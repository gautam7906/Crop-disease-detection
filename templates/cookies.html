1. Introduction

Welcome to AgriCare's Cookies Policy. This policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website and mobile applications to provide and improve our services, enhance your user experience, and analyze how our services are used.

By using AgriCare's services, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy, unless you have disabled them in your browser settings.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about the technologies we use. This policy provides detailed information about the types of cookies we use, their purposes, and how you can control them.

2. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

How Cookies Work

When you visit our website, we may place cookies on your device. These cookies allow us to recognize your device on subsequent visits and remember your preferences and actions over time. Each cookie is unique to your web browser and contains anonymous information that allows us to enhance your browsing experience.

Types of Cookies Based on Duration

  • Session Cookies: These are temporary cookies that remain on your device until you close your browser. They are essential for navigating our website and using its features.
  • Persistent Cookies: These cookies remain on your device for a set period specified in the cookie (usually between a few days and several years). They remember your preferences and actions across multiple browsing sessions.

Other Tracking Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use other similar technologies such as:

  • Web Beacons: Small graphic images embedded in web pages or emails that help us analyze user behavior.
  • Local Storage: Technology that allows websites to store information locally on your device.
  • Scripts: Small pieces of code that enable specific functionalities on our website.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

AgriCare uses several types of cookies for different purposes. We classify them as follows:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. You cannot disable these cookies as the website cannot function properly without them.

Examples: User authentication, maintaining user session state, preventing fraudulent use of login credentials.

Analytics/Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This data helps us improve how our website works.

Examples: Counting visits and traffic sources, measuring which pages are most popular, understanding how visitors move around the site.

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization based on your preferences and past activity. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages.

Examples: Remembering your language preference, region selection, or username for future visits.

Targeting/Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to track visitors across websites to display relevant advertisements. They are also used to limit the number of times you see an ad and measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

Examples: Cookies placed by advertising networks to collect information about your browsing habits and interests.

Social Media Cookies

These cookies are set by social media services that we have added to our site to enable you to share our content with your friends and networks. They are capable of tracking your browser across other sites and building a profile of your interests.

6. Cookie Duration

Cookies remain on your device for different periods of time depending on their type:

Session Cookies

These cookies are temporary and are deleted automatically when you close your browser. They are essential for the proper functioning of our website during your visit.

Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a predetermined period. The duration varies depending on the purpose of the cookie:

  • Authentication cookies: Typically remain for the duration of your session or for a few days for "remember me" functionality
  • Analytics cookies: May remain for periods ranging from 30 minutes to 2 years
  • Advertising cookies: Often have longer durations, sometimes up to several years
  • Preference cookies: Usually remain for extended periods to remember your settings

The exact duration for each cookie is specified in the detailed cookie table below.

7. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways. Please note that removing or blocking cookies may impact your user experience and parts of our website may no longer be fully accessible.

Browser Controls

Most browsers allow you to view, manage, delete, and block cookies for a website. You can usually find these settings in the "Options" or "Preferences" menu of your browser. Be aware that if you delete all cookies, any preferences you have set will be lost.

Browser-Specific Instructions

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Advanced → Privacy and security → Content settings → Cookies
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Internet Explorer: Tools → Internet Options → Privacy
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies

Do Not Track

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" feature that lets you tell websites that you do not want to be tracked. Our website does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" signals, as there is no industry standard for implementation.

Opt-Out Tools

For advertising cookies, you can opt out of many third-party advertising networks through the following resources:

  • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA): http://optout.aboutads.info/
  • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI): http://optout.networkadvertising.org/
  • European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance (EDAA): http://www.youronlinechoices.com/
